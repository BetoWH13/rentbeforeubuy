Disclaimer & Affiliate Disclosure

Educational decision-support only: what this site is, what it is not, why we do not quote prices or recommend specific brands, and how affiliate booking links work.

What this site is

Drive Before Buy is an independent educational decision-support website for car shoppers. The framework is built around a single idea: rent a similar vehicle for a weekend before buying, to test whether the platform and category fit your real life. The core artifact is the Weekend Test Drive Checklist, supported by category pages and a cost-vs-remorse calculator.

The site is owned and operated as an editorial project. It is not owned by, controlled by, or affiliated with any car manufacturer, dealership, rental company, insurance provider, or financial institution, other than the affiliate relationship described below.

The content is for general educational and informational purposes only. It is not individualized buying advice, legal advice, financial advice, tax advice, insurance advice, or mechanical advice. It is meant to help you ask better questions before you rent, inspect, finance, lease, or buy a vehicle.

  • Not professional advice. Nothing on this site is legal, financial, tax, insurance, mechanical, or medical advice. We are not lawyers, financial advisors, tax professionals, mechanics, or doctors. We do not know your specific situation.
  • Not a car dealer or broker. We do not sell cars. We do not represent dealers. We do not negotiate on your behalf. We do not receive commissions from car sales.
  • Not a rental company. We do not rent vehicles. We do not control rental inventory, pricing, or vehicle assignment. We link to third-party rental comparison platforms; the rental itself is between you and the rental company.
  • Not a review site. We do not claim to have hands-on tested specific year/trim combinations. We do not publish "best overall" rankings. We do not pretend to be Edmunds, Consumer Reports, or any other publication that does long-term vehicle testing.
  • Not a substitute for an independent inspection. For used and CPO purchases, an independent pre-purchase inspection by a qualified mechanic is essential. See the used/CPO page. The rental test does not replace the inspection.
  • Not a substitute for professional buying advice. If your situation involves tax questions (especially around EV tax credits), financing, lease termination, or insurance, talk to a qualified professional. We do not give that advice.

Rental platforms, manufacturers, dealers, lenders, and insurers do not review or approve our editorial content before publication, do not select the topics we cover, and do not see drafts.

Affiliate links are marked at the point where they appear. Affiliate compensation does not change our checklist, category guidance, calculator, or methodology.

The site does not control rental prices, availability, vehicle assignment, insurance terms, cancellation policies, or customer support. Your rental contract is with the rental company or booking platform, not with this site.
